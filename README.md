# Take Five

Be well! Do you hyperfocus? Does time slip away, while you are focused on deep work?

If so, this is the app for you! Take Five is a mobile app that helps us builld healthy micro habbits. Simply define a cadence during working hours and take five will take care of the rest, gently nudging you to do a variety of tasks at random. 

This app is designed to be deployed to multiple platforms and values simplicity over all else.
